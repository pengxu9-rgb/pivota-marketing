The Pivota brand

One letter, one stop, one system.

Pivota's identity is a lowercase p set in Fredoka SemiBold, closed with a clean circular period. The bowl is warm and geometric; the dot is the moment of stop. Together they read as a complete thought.

This kit is the production brand standard — everything you need to ship Pivota into any surface. Drop pivota-brand.css into a page, link the SVG or PNG, and you're consistent with the rest of the system.

01 / The mark

Four worlds. One letter.

The mark lives in one of four background worlds — gradient, purple, ink, paper. The first three render the glyph white; paper renders it ink. There is no other approved background.

04 / Type

Three families. Three jobs.

Fredoka for the logo. Inter for the product. Cormorant for the editorial moments. Don't mix the jobs.
Brand
Pivota.
font-family: Fredoka
weight: 600 (SemiBold)
tracking: −0.02em
role: logo + wordmark only
never: body, UI, headlines
Editorial
A moment of pause.
font-family: Cormorant Garamond
weight: 400, 500, 600, 700
italic available
role: hero headlines, magazine sections, long-form intros
Product
Section heading
Pivota body copy sits at 13 px. The system is dense, intentionally — 13, not 14. Long-form pages may step up to 14 or 16.
font-family: Inter
weight: 300, 400, 500, 600, 700, 800, 900
role: all product UI, headings, body, labels
